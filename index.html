Pick the right place

Do your research first. People with either great or bad experience the most will definitely leave reviews about restaurants.

Know what you are eating

You are what you eat! Although you are in charge of your life, be mindful of what you consume. Is it healthy? How is the sugar content on this one?

Find food buddies

Eating with your squad definitely makes a huge difference when eating! This is because you get to enjoy your food and also the company of others.
